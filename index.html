My first app

SpacExtra

SpacExtra is your one-stop-shop for all things SpaceX - the world's most innovative, privatised space company! You can track the upcoming launches, learn about the SpaceX fleet, and countdown to T-0 on your Home Screen!

Main features:

  • Countdown to T-0 either in-app, or right from your home screen!
  • Get clued up on the latest information about the fleet
  • Read about where the rockets launch and land from
  • Quick access to Wikipedia articles about crew members

Coming soon:
  • Notifications, so you can be the first to know about the upcoming launches
  • Siri integration, so you can get information as fast as a rocket
  • Custom calendar, to keep track of upcoming launches
  • Apple Watch complications
